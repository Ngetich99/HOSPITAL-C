Our Services

At Cannan Hospital and Wellness Centre, we believe that every individual deserves exceptiona l healthcare delivered with compassion, expertise, and a personal touch. Established with a vision to be a beacon of health and wellness in our community, our hospital is committed to providing comprehensive , patient-centered care in a nurturing environment. .

Theatre

Our theatre services complement our diagnostic offerings with a range of surgical procedures performed by our experienced surgeons. From minor procedures to complex surgeries, our team is dedicated to providing exceptional care and achieving the best possible outcomes for our patients.

Laboratory

Our laboratory is fully accredited as a Class E facility by the KMTTLB, offering a wide range of diagnostic tests including routine, specialized, and advanced molecular analyses, ensuring the highest standards of accuracy and reliability in patient care.".

Dental clinic

Our Dental Clinic is dedicated to providing top-quality oral health care in a comfortable and welcoming environment. Our team of experienced dentists and dental professionals is committed to offering comprehensive dental services tailored to meet the unique needs of each patient.

Nursing and special Care

Our nursing team is at the heart of our patient care services, embodying our commitment to delivering compassionate, high-quality healthcare. Our dedicated nurses play a pivotal role in ensuring that every patient receives personalized attention and exceptional care throughout their stay.

Maternity care

we are dedicated to providing comprehensive and compassionate maternity care, ensuring a safe and supportive experience for mothers and their newborns. Our maternity services are designed to offer exceptional care throughout your pregnancy, labor, and delivery, as well as in the crucial postpartum period

24 Hour Service

our commitment to patient care extends around the clock with our dedicated 24-hour emergency care services. We understand that medical emergencies can arise at any time, and we are here to provide immediate, expert care whenever you need it.
